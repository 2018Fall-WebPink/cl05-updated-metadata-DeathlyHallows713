Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

The best sandbox game of all time right now is Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild! It's available on the Wii U and the Nintendo Switch, and fans of the Legend of Zelda series are raving about it! It's all about a hero who is destined for great things, but has lost his memory due to an almost fatal injury and can't remember what it is he is destined to do. You must play as this hero and regain your memories, all the while fighting a battle between light and dark!

Breath of the Wild has got critics wild about the stunning graphics and incredibly fun gameplay. Nintendo has completely knocked it out of the park this time!

"The gameplay is fantastic, and the graphics are absolutely gorgeous! Five stars!" -Susie Spring
"I'm comepletely absorbed by the storyline! I can't stop playing it!" -James Johnson
